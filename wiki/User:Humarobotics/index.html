Organization

Generation Robots Formed in 2008 and based in Bordeaux, France, Génération Robots is a leading European company in the distribution of service robots and related accessories, sensors and development software. With its tri-lingual website and customers all over Europe, Génération Robots has become a favourite source of pioneering projects for robot enthusiasts. Génération Robots is also very involved in the academic arena (universities and similar institutions) and in education in general (secondary schools) across Europe. For more information about Génération Robots, please visit http://www.generationrobots.com

Humarobotics HumaRobotics is the brand name for services offered by Génération Robots. Through this brand, Génération Robots R&D staff offer their expertise in robot programming and artificial intelligence, plus a range of training courses intended for the education and business sectors alike. HumaRobotics’ skills are mainly focussed on robotics, embedded systems, artificial intelligence, advanced algorithms and artificial vision. For more information about HumaRobotics, please visit http://www.humarobotics.com

Position

Projects

Baxter_Tasker : an interactive Pick and Place program developed using the Baxter ROS SDK. Using this project, you'll be able to program simple and advanced pick and place, make some useful demos of learning by demonstration and generate code templates that you'll be able to customize. To know more, go to the Showcase section of this wiki.
